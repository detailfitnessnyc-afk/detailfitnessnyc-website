About

Terrence Powell is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer, Precision Nutrition Level 1 Coach, and a Focus Personal Training Institute graduate. He brings over four years of corporate gym experience and over ten years of private training across NYC.

Training here is built around movement quality first — corrective exercise, joint-friendly strength training, and smart progression — so you move better, feel better, and perform at a higher level.

For clients who want a clear system, Terrence delivers a structured 12-week transformation model (Phase 1: Foundation, Phase 2: Strength, Phase 3: Performance) with a plan that fits a busy schedule and produces measurable results.
